* Unpacked BCD
    * 4 LSB's are used.
    * 4 MSB's are kept 0.
    * Eg. 4 = 0000 0100
* Packed BCD
    * 4 LSB's = One number
    * 4 MSB's = Second number
    * Eg. 24 = 0010 0100
